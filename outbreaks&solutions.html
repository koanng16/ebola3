    Outbreaks and Solutions of Ebola

    Based on the WHO toll, Ebola outbreaks includes about 2,240 cases and 1,229 deaths, by far the most worst outbreak of Ebola in its history. It spanning into 4 countries just in West Africa. According to WHO, so far there are no sign of Ebola slowing. Even with such high death toll, there are currently no vaccines, drugs or cure for Ebola. Although, there are a couple of scientist experimenting for vaccines. The one main problem that is holding scientist back from actually publishing the vaccine is money. Developing and testing a product for human use may run up to billions of dollars.
